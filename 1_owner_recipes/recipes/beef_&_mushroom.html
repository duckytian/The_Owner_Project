Beef & Mushroom Pizza

Savory pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, seasoned beef, and earthy mushrooms, often complemented by vegetables like bell peppers or onions for added flavor

Ingredients:

Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Toppings:

  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, salt, sugar, and yeast. Add warm water and olive oil, then mix until a soft dough forms.
  2. Knead on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough in a bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and ground beef, then cook for 5–7 minutes until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In the same pan, sauté the sliced mushrooms and onions for 2–3 minutes until slightly soft.
  6. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  7. Roll or stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle and place it on a pizza pan or baking tray.
  8. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about 1 inch around the edge.
  9. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese, then add the cooked ground beef and mushrooms evenly on top.
  10. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust becomes golden and the cheese melts.
  11. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve.
