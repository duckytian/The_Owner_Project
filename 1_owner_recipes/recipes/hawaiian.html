Hawaiian Pizza

Savory dish topped with tomato sauce, cheese, ham, and sweet pineapple chunks, blending salty and sweet flavors in one bite.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Toppings:

  • 1 piece pizza dough
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1–2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup ham, sliced or diced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned, drained)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning (optional)

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Place the pizza dough on a pizza pan or baking tray. Stretch or roll it to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread pizza (tomato) sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small space around the edges for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Place ham slices or diced ham and pineapple chunks evenly on top of the cheese.
  6. Put the pizza in the oven and bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust becomes golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
